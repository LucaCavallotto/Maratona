**Maratona** is a comprehensive, minimalist running calculator designed to help runners of all levels plan their training and race strategies. Built with a "Braun ET 55 / iOS" inspired aesthetic, it offers a clean, dark-themed interface for distraction-free calculations.

## Features

Maratona includes five essential tools for runners:

### 1. Training Zones Calculator
Input your recent best 10K time to generate personalized training zones (Z1 to Z7). This tool also provides race predictions for 5K, 10K, Half Marathon, and Marathon distances based on your threshold pace.

### 2. Pace Calculator
Calculate your average pace based on a given distance and total time. Includes quick presets for common race distances (5K, 10K, HM, Marathon) and generates 1km splits.

### 3. Time Calculator
Determine your total race time based on a target distance and pace. Useful for planning race goals or estimating finish times.

### 4. Distance Calculator
Find out how far you can run in a specific time at a given pace. Perfect for time-based training sessions.

### 5. Km-Mile Converter
Instantly convert distances and paces between kilometers and miles.

## Additional Functionality

-   **Race Predictions:** Estimates your potential performance across various distances.
-   **Split Times:** Automatically generates 1km split tables for pace, time, and distance calculations.
-   **Copy Results:** One-click button to copy your calculation results to the clipboard for easy sharing or logging.
-   **Responsive Design:** optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.
